Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: May 2026

AlternateIncomeSource.com believes in full transparency. This page explains clearly and honestly how this website may earn income through affiliate relationships — and what that means for you as a visitor.

1. What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a way for website owners to earn a commission by recommending products or services. Here is how it works:

  • This website includes links to external products or services
  • These links contain a unique tracking code
  • If you click the link and make a purchase or sign up, this website earns a small commission
  • You pay exactly the same price — the commission comes from the company, not from you
  • There is never any extra cost to you for using an affiliate link

2. Our Commitment to Honesty

💯 The honest truth about recommendations on this site

Every product or service recommended on AlternateIncomeSource.com is something that has been personally researched, used, or carefully evaluated. We only recommend things we genuinely believe provide real value. We never recommend something simply because it pays a higher commission. If a product is not good, it will not appear on this site — regardless of what it pays.

3. FTC Compliance (United States)

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines in the United States, we disclose that this website may receive compensation for recommendations and links to products or services. This disclosure applies to all content on this website including blog posts, guides, tool descriptions, and any other pages.

When a page contains affiliate links, it will include a disclosure notice. The existence of affiliate relationships does not influence our editorial content or recommendations.

4. EU and UK Compliance

For visitors from the European Union and United Kingdom, we confirm that commercial relationships are disclosed in accordance with applicable consumer protection regulations. Any content that is influenced by a commercial relationship is clearly identified as such.

5. Which Affiliate Programs May Be Used

This website may participate in affiliate programs including but not limited to:

  • Web hosting services — for visitors wanting to build their own websites
  • Freelance platforms — for visitors wanting to earn through skills
  • Online learning platforms — for visitors wanting to develop new skills
  • Digital tools and software — tools that complement the free tools on this site
  • Amazon Associates — for relevant product recommendations

Specific affiliate partners will be added to this page as they are introduced on the website.

6. Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense is not an affiliate program — it is an advertising network. We earn income when visitors view or click advertisements. Google AdSense relationships are governed separately under our Privacy Policy.

7. No Extra Cost to You

Using an affiliate link on this website costs you nothing extra. The price you pay for any product or service is exactly the same whether you use our affiliate link or navigate directly to the company's website. In some cases, our affiliate relationship may even result in special discounts being available to you.

8. Your Trust Comes First

The primary purpose of AlternateIncomeSource.com is to provide genuinely useful free tools, honest earning information, and valuable educational content. Affiliate income is a secondary revenue source that helps keep the website free and running. We will never compromise the quality or honesty of our content for affiliate income.

If you ever feel that a recommendation on this site is not genuine or that a commercial relationship has not been properly disclosed, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Disclosure

This Affiliate Disclosure may be updated as new affiliate relationships are established. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

📧 Contact

Questions about affiliate relationships or specific recommendations? Contact us through www.AlternateIncomeSource.com. We respond to all genuine enquiries within 30 days.
