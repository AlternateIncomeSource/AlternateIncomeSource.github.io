What is Swagbucks?

Swagbucks rewards you with points called SB for completing small online activities — answering daily polls, completing surveys, playing browser games, and making purchases through their shopping portal. Once you collect 530 SB you can withdraw $5 via PayPal or exchange for gift cards.

The daily poll is the simplest task. It takes 10 seconds and earns you 1 SB every single day automatically. Surveys pay more but they may disqualify you — however even when disqualified, Swagbucks usually credits a small number of SB for your time.

Realistic expectation: Surveys and tasks on Swagbucks can earn you $10-30 per month with consistent daily effort. The signup bonus — available in many countries — gives you a head start. Check if the bonus is available in your country after signing up.

💡 Quick first target: Earn 300 SB within your first 30 days. That is achievable easily through the daily poll plus a few short surveys. This unlocks your signup bonus in eligible countries.
